Members & Shareholders

Gin & Luck is supported by a growing community of investors and members who believe in what we’re building.

They aren’t just guests — they’re active participants in our ecosystem. Some have participated in our past crowdfunding rounds, while others engage more deeply through our new membership program, and receive specific access, perks, and experiences across our venues.

This guide aims to provide a clear overview of who they are and how to best take care of them.

Why Membership?

Membership is a natural extension of our marketing flywheel—deepening relationships with our most engaged guests while creating meaningful, recurring connection with the brand.

Rooted in access, community, and value, it’s our way of saying thank you—while inviting more frequent visits and bringing Death & Co into more moments of our guests’ lives.

This is not another exclusive club. It’s an invite-only, brand-aligned community—an evolution of our fan-owned model that celebrates the people who have helped build Death & Co.

Who They Are

Shareholders

  • Gin & Luck is community-driven and employee-aligned, with ownership shared across thousands of fan shareholders and team members.
  • We prefer to use the term “shareholder” vs investor (but you may see either used Interchangeably)
  • Shareholders represent a range of investment levels from $1k to $1M+
  • Shareholders have a similar suite of perks when they visit, however their primary long term benefit is an eventual return on their investment
  • Note - some shareholders have opted into membership as well

How We Treat Them

Hospitality Expectations

For All Members and Shareholders
  1. Treat this group as our community – they are our biggest fans and supporters!
  2. Refer to them by name after they are idenfitied – via their membership card or reservation.
  3. Alert MOD upon arrival and seating.
  4. Table touches: MOD to introduce themselves and check in at least once during the visit. All team is encouraged to extend a hospitable welcome, and to use their discretion in adds to the guest’s experience (welcome splash, snacks, shots, etc).

Thank you for your support!

Members Only

House Accounts

When using a House Account:

  1. Make sure to apply “Member – 10% Off” discount to the entire check. All members are subject to this discount!

Lightspeed Users, please remember, all check names must include the Member’s full name in order to be processed correctly

  1. Make sure to apply a 20% Gratuity to the entire check. All House Accounts charges are subject to an automatic 20% gratuity!
  2. To close out the bill, select “House Account” tender type in your POS — see specific instructions below:

If a guest is a Member, but their name does not appear in the “House Accounts” tender type, please notify your MOD immediately!

No check should be dropped at the end of the experience — just make sure it is properly accounted for in the system. Our intent is to provide a smooth & effortless experience for our Members!

In Toast, all Members should have unique named accounts under “House Accounts” in your POS. If a Member’s name does not appear under the House Accounts tab, please confirm they are a Member not merely an Investor.

In Lightspeed, all transactions can be rung under the same tender — “Community Member”.

Please note: the full check should be closed out to the member’s house account. We have their credit card on file that we charge should their bill exceed their current house account credit.

When in doubt, charge everything to the house account! Do not take additional payment.
